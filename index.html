Camellia rusticana

a species of the genus Camellia, belonging to the family Theaceae, also called the "Rose of Winter".  Every spring this flower is celebrated in California at the Camellia Flower Festival. During one of these blooming springs Camille Wagstaff was born and met her namesake. At a very young age a relationship with plants was made as she learned to garden from her parents. A desire grew to work with plants. This passion has become a profession, an education with emphasis on ethnobotany, and a hope for a more holistic future in global land management and stronger local ecological sovereignty.
