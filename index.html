Camellia rusticana

a species of the genus Camellia, belonging to the family Theaceae. Sometimes called the rose of winter.  Every spring this flower is celebrated in Sacramento, CA as the Camellia Flower Festival. During one of these springs Camille was born and named. At a very young age a strong connection with plants was made and a desire to grow and work with them. This passion has become a profession, an education, and a hope for more holistic and natural medicine with emphasis on ethnobotany and land management.
